Web Design

Web design is the process of planning, creating, and maintaining websites. It encompasses a broad range of disciplines, including graphic design, user experience (UX) design, and interface (UI) design. Web designers work to create websites that are both visually appealing and easy to use.

Responsive Web Design is an approach to designing web content that appears regardless of the resolution governed by the device. It's typically accomplished with viewport breakpoints (resolution cut-offs for when content scales to that view). The viewports should adjust logically on tablets, phones, and desktops of any resolution.
